How much profit per dollar deposited?
Same savings. One difference: selling.

Think of your exchange account: your strategy balance is everything inside it — BTC plus the USDT from any sales, waiting for the next green zone. You keep saving every period regardless. The question is the simplest one in investing: what % profit did your deposited money make?

The model, stated plainly: you keep saving on schedule — your deposit each period is the base amount × the Gresham Index multiplier (that's fresh money, even in green zones). Cash from sales never leaves: it stays in your balance as USDT and redeploys on top of your deposits when the strategy says so. Smart DCA and Full Cycle deposit exactly the same money — the entire gap between them is the value of selling tops and recycling. For the fixed-budget comparison, see simulator #1.
